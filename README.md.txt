## ðŸ“˜ Project Overview
This project investigated **Pseudomonas aeruginosa biofilm matrix architecture** in murine skin infections, focusing on the spatial distribution of the exopolysaccharide **Psl** across three genotypes:
- **A:** Wild-type (WT)  
- **B:** Deletion (DL)  
- **C:** Constitutive Expression (CE)

Fluorescence microscopy and image-based quantification were used to measure Psl mean intensities at infection and distant tissue sites.  
Statistical analysis included **ANOVA** and **two-way repeated-measures ANOVA** with post-hoc tests to determine group- and site-level differences.
